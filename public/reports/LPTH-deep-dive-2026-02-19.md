## 1. Business Overview

### What They Do
LightPath Technologies is a designer, manufacturer, and distributor of optical and infrared components, serving as a vertically integrated provider of thermal imaging solutions. The company operates through three main product segments:

- **Precision Molded Optics:** Aspheric lenses, fused fiber collimators, GRADIUM® lenses
- **Infrared Products:** Molded and turned infrared lenses using proprietary BlackDiamond™ chalcogenide glass materials
- **Assemblies & Camera Systems:** Complete thermal imaging assemblies and G5 Infrared camera systems (post-acquisition)

### Key Technology Advantage
**BlackDiamond™ Glass:** LightPath holds an exclusive license from the U.S. Naval Research Laboratory for proprietary chalcogenide-based glass materials. This provides a critical competitive moat, especially as Chinese restrictions on germanium exports have created supply chain vulnerabilities for competitors.

### Market Position
LightPath serves diverse end markets including:
- **Defense (Primary):** Border security, night vision, missile defense systems
- **Commercial:** Automotive safety, medical devices, industrial sensors, telecommunications
- **Law Enforcement:** Counter-UAS systems, surveillance applications

### Recent Strategic Transformation
The February 2025 acquisition of G5 Infrared LLC significantly expanded LightPath's capabilities from component supplier to complete camera systems provider. G5 brings established defense contracts and multi-year programs of record in shipboard surveillance, border security, and counter-UAS systems.

### Competitive Landscape
**Key Competitors:**
- **Large Players:** Coherent Corp., Lumentum Holdings (broader photonics portfolios but less thermal imaging focus)
- **Niche Players:** Optimax Systems, Knight Optical (UK), Janos Technology, Abrisa Technologies
- **Advantage:** LightPath's vertical integration + BlackDiamond exclusivity + U.S.-based supply chain creates differentiation in defense applications
