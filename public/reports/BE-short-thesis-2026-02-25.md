# Bloom Energy (BE) Short Thesis Report
**Date:** February 25, 2026  
**Current Price:** $176.22 (+686% YTD)  
**Analyst Consensus Target:** $103.23 (-41.4%)

---

## Executive Summary

Bloom Energy is trading at extreme valuations (P/B 60x, EV/EBITDA 339x) following a monster rally driven by AI data center power demand. While the thesis has substance—Brookfield $5B deal, 1GW AEP contract, Oracle partnership—the stock has priced in perfection and then some. Insiders have been aggressively selling into the strength, including a $276M block sale by 10% owner SK ecoplant. The risk/reward for puts is asymmetric but IV is elevated (~117%), making options expensive.

**Verdict:** Modestly bearish. The short thesis has merit, but timing is tricky and squeeze risk exists if momentum continues.
