## Executive Summary

Aptiv is a Tier-1 automotive supplier focused on advanced driver assistance systems (ADAS), connected car technology, and electrical architecture. The company is undergoing a strategic transformation: spinning off its lower-margin Electrical Distribution Systems (EDS) business by March 2026 and taking a $648M impairment on Wind River. Post-separation, "RemainCo" will be a higher-margin, technology-focused business with growing non-auto exposure.

**Thesis Fit:** ⚠️ Mixed. AI integration in vehicles is real, but EV slowdown and automotive cyclicality are headwinds. International exposure is significant.

## Conclusion

**Rating: SPECULATIVE BUY**

APTV is a show-me story. The EDS spin-off could unlock significant value, and post-separation RemainCo will be a higher-quality business. However, Wind River impairment, Motional losses, and automotive cyclicality create uncertainty.

**Bull case:** EDS spin creates $22B+ combined value, ADAS adoption accelerates, RemainCo trades at 12x EBITDA → $100+ stock.

**Bear case:** Auto production cuts, EV slowdown persists, EDS sale falls through → $50-60 stock.

**Best Entry:** Wait for EDS clarity. If spin completes cleanly by Q1 2026, RemainCo could be an attractive mid-cap tech-auto play.

**Position sizing:** Smaller position given cyclical risk and transformation uncertainty.
