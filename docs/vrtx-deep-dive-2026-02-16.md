## ðŸ¢ **1. The Business - Biotechnology Royalty**

### **Core Franchise: Cystic Fibrosis Dominance**
Vertex isn't just a biotech company - they ARE cystic fibrosis. With **five approved CFTR modulators**, they treat the underlying cause of CF in ~95% of patients across 60+ countries. This isn't market share; this is market ownership.

**The Portfolio:**
- **Trikafta/Kaftrio** - The crown jewel, treats most common CF mutations
- **Symdeko/Symkevi** - Combination therapy for specific mutations  
- **Orkambi** - Earlier generation but still revenue-generating
- **Kalydeco** - The OG that started it all
- **ALYFTREK** - Newest addition expanding coverage

### **Revolutionary Gene Therapy Platform**
**CASGEVY (CTX001)** - The world's first approved CRISPR/Cas9 gene-editing therapy. This isn't incrementally better - this is potentially curative.

**Approved Indications:**
- Sickle Cell Disease (SCD) - FDA approved Dec 2023
- Transfusion-Dependent Beta Thalassemia (TDT) - FDA approved Jan 2024

**Infrastructure:** Nine Authorized Treatment Centers (ATCs) operational in US, expanding globally with recent Saudi Arabia approval.

### **Expanding Therapeutic Universe**
Beyond CF and gene therapy, VRTX is building positions in:
- **Pain Management** - JOURNAVX (non-opioid), targeting $8B+ market
- **APOL1-Mediated Kidney Disease** - Massive underserved population
- **Duchenne Muscular Dystrophy** - Gene therapy approach
- **Alpha-1 Antitrypsin Deficiency** - Leveraging CFTR expertise
- **Type 1 Diabetes** - Long-term gene therapy play
