Fair value? In my book, even with the AI premium, this should trade at 18-22x earnings max. That puts us around $160-200 per share, not $268.

**Risk Assessment:** Three things keep me up at night with CEG. First, regulatory risk - one Three Mile Island-style incident and this thing craters 50% overnight. Second, the customer concentration is getting dangerous - too much eggs in the Big Tech basket. Third, and this is the biggie - uranium costs and waste disposal are ticking time bombs on the balance sheet. When these plants start decomissioning in 20-30 years, those liabilities could eat decades of profits.

**The Bottom Line:** CEG has a great business at a terrible price. The AI narrative is real, but the market's already paid for the next decade of growth. This is classic "cigar butt" territory - there's one good puff left, but you're paying Cuban prices for it.

**My Grade: C+ with LOW conviction**

Could work if AI power demand goes parabolic, but the risk-reward is upside down. I'd rather wait for a 40% haircut or find a cheaper way to play the nuclear renaissance. Sometimes the best investment decision is the one you don't make.
