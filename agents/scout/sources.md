## Screening Criteria

### For Longs (Fastest Horses)
- Relative strength vs sector and market
- Improving fundamentals (margins, FCF)
- Institutional accumulation
- Positive catalyst pipeline
- Breaking out of bases on volume

### For Shorts (Weakest Dogs)
- Relative weakness vs sector
- Deteriorating fundamentals
- Insider selling
- Negative catalysts ahead
- Breaking down from distribution
